Maru

Maru is a Scottish Fold from Japan who achieved worldwide fame for his love of boxes. No box is too small for Maru to attempt squeezing into, delighting millions of viewers.

With over 300 million YouTube views, Maru holds the Guinness World Record for most watched animal on YouTube.

Get notified about Maru's newest box adventures and special videos.

Nyancat

Nyancat is the legendary Pop-Tart cat who flies through space leaving a rainbow trail. This animated meme became one of the most iconic internet phenomena of the 2010s.

Grumpy Cat

Grumpy Cat, whose real name was Tardar Sauce, became famous for her permanently grumpy facial expression. She inspired countless memes and brought joy to millions despite her perpetual frown.

Lil Bub

Lil Bub was a special needs cat known for her unique appearance and big personality. She used her fame to raise money for special needs pets and animal charities across the country.

Celebrate Lil Bub's legacy and support special needs animals in her honor.
